Sounds of San Anto: A Statistical Analysis of San Antonio's Music Scene

Erick Lima | Community Engaged Digital Scholarship Hub (CEDISH)
This project explores the Sounds of San Anto dataset through a exploratory analysis lense. The goal of this project is to do a surface level analysis of the data set and look any potential connections with gentrification, rent increase, displacement, curator bias etc. It uses R to analyze data and generate insights related to the Sounds of San Anto project (Product 1).
